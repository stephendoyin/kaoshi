P2P Swap
What is the P2P swap?

P2P swaps are a remittance service guaranteed by the banks in your home/destination country. Banks swap the outbound cross-border needs of their customers with your inbound remittance request, thus avoiding the need to send money across borders. You make payment for a bank to its customer’s beneficiary in your country, and, in exchange, the bank makes a payment to your beneficiary in the home country. No money crosses the border.

Why is P2P swap cheaper?

P2P swap is cheaper because it circumvents the need to send money across borders. Since money is locally swapped in each jurisdiction, the traditional expensive fees of cross-border transfer and currency conversion do not apply.

How secure is P2P Swap?

P2P swaps are offered by the highly regulated banks in your home countries. They are very secure.

Is the only payment method for P2P swap bank transfer? Can I use my debit/credit card for the P2P swap?

All P2P swaps can only be paid for by bank transfers.

Can I cancel a P2P swap offer after selecting it?

Canceling a P2P swap or failing to make payment for a P2P swap after you have selected it could result in an account suspension and fine.

Can I cancel a P2P swap after making payment?

No, all transactions are final. Consider P2P swaps cash payments. Once paid, the payment cannot be reversed.
